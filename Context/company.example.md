## Company overview
**Name:** NestWise
**Stage:** Series A ($9M raised, 14 months post-launch)
**Industry:** Home Services / Local Commerce / Marketplace
**Mission / one-liner:** Help homeowners instantly find reliable, fairly-priced local pros for any home job — and book them in under 10 minutes.

---

## Product
**Core product:** Mobile-first app (iOS + Android) + web. Homeowners discover vetted local service professionals ranked by real reviews, response time, and job completion rate. Finding a reliable pro is the primary job; the app then surfaces upfront pricing ranges and enables direct booking without back-and-forth messages.

**Primary value prop:** The most reliable local pros for your home, with transparent pricing and confirmed bookings in under 10 minutes.

**What we are, in order of priority:**
1. Pro discovery — vetted, ranked, job-type filtered, locally relevant
2. Booking layer — confirmed appointments without back-and-forth scheduling
3. Trust layer — verified reviews, job history, and response rate signals

**Business model:**
- Transaction fees from completed bookings (primary, ~70% of revenue)
- Pro subscription "NestWise Pro" — $49/mo, unlocks priority placement, analytics dashboard, and lead insights (~20% of revenue)
- Featured placement for high-volume pros (~10% of revenue)

## Team & org
**Company size:** 38 full-time
**Eng size:** 14 (FE, BE, mobile, data)
**PM org size:** 2 PMs (1 lead + 1 IC)
**Key orgs that touch product decisions:** Engineering, Design, Data/Analytics, Marketing/Growth, Partnerships (pro supply), Legal, Customer Success

## Current focus
**Top company OKR this quarter:** Grow Monthly Active Homeowners (MAH) from 180K → 260K by end of Q3
**Top product OKR this quarter:** Increase booking completion rate from 34% → 50% by reducing scheduling friction and improving pro response time

---

## Constraints & context
- Pro supply is geo-dependent — product experience degrades significantly outside top 30 metro areas; expansion to smaller markets is a known gap
- Pro availability data is inconsistent — some pros update in real time, others have 24–48 hour lag; shown availability is not always accurate
- Privacy: we do not sell user or pro behavioral data; all personalization is within our own infrastructure
- Legal reviews any feature involving pricing commitments or satisfaction guarantees
- iOS/Android feature parity is a persistent pain point — iOS ships ~2 weeks ahead of Android due to team size
- Pro payment system has technical debt; payout timing has had 2 incidents in the past 4 months causing pro complaints
