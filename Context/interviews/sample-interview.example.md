# sample-interview.example.md
# Participant: Jordan T. | Date: February 14, 2025 | Topic: Pro discovery and booking friction
# Interviewer: Nina Patel (Head of Customer Success)
# Duration: 38 minutes
# Recruitment: Recruited from NestWise app, 2+ bookings in last 6 months

---

## Raw transcript

**Nina:** Thanks for making time today Jordan. Can you walk me through the last time you needed to find a plumber?

**Jordan:** Yeah so this was maybe three weeks ago. We had a slow drain in the bathroom and I kept putting it off because honestly I just dread the whole process of finding someone. Like I don't know who to trust.

**Nina:** What makes it hard to know who to trust?

**Jordan:** I mean reviews help but you can't always tell if they're real. And then you get a quote and you have no idea if it's reasonable. I just want someone to show up when they say they will and not charge me double what they quoted.

**Nina:** Tell me more about the quote piece. What happened with the plumber you ended up booking?

**Jordan:** So I found this guy through NestWise actually. His profile looked good, lots of reviews. He gave me a range — said somewhere between $150 and $300 depending on what he found. I booked him. He showed up on time which was honestly already better than I expected. Charged me $240 which felt okay but I still don't really know if that was fair.

**Nina:** What would have made you feel more confident about the price?

**Jordan:** I don't know. Maybe if I could see what other people paid for similar jobs? Like a benchmark or something. Even just knowing the average would help. Right now I'm just guessing.

**Nina:** How did you find NestWise originally?

**Jordan:** My neighbor used it. She said she'd had good luck. I think I'd tried Yelp before and just had a bad experience — guy didn't show up, didn't call. So I was pretty skeptical coming in.

**Nina:** What would make you recommend NestWise to someone else?

**Jordan:** Honestly the showing up on time thing is huge. If I knew that every pro on the platform had like a reliability score or something — not just stars but specifically did they show up and did they do it in the timeframe — I'd feel way better recommending it.

**Nina:** Is there anything that almost stopped you from booking that time?

**Jordan:** The price range thing. $150 to $300 is a big range. If it had said $300 to $600 I probably would have gotten a second quote. But I also didn't want to spend another hour doing that. I just want someone to show up when they say they will and not rip me off. That's it. That's all I want.

---

## Interviewer notes (raw)

- Jordan is a homeowner of 4 years, bought first home age 31
- Clear trust deficit from prior bad experience on Yelp
- Price transparency is the #1 concern — wants benchmarks not just ranges
- Reliability (showing up on time) mentioned twice unprompted — strong signal
- Social proof via neighbor referral was the acquisition trigger
- Did not mention the NestWise app UI at all — entirely focused on the pro experience
- Follow-up question: would he pay for a "guaranteed response time" feature?
