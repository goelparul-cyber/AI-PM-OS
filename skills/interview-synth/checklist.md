# interview-synth checklist

Run through every item before marking the synthesis complete.

## Coverage
- [ ] Every major topic the interviewer raised has a corresponding theme or raw signal entry
- [ ] Participant's own framing is preserved — synthesis doesn't over-interpret
- [ ] Contradictions within the interview are noted, not smoothed over
- [ ] Silence / non-answers are logged if meaningful

## Quotes
- [ ] Every quote is verbatim — no paraphrasing inside quotation marks
- [ ] Every quote includes a line number or timestamp reference
- [ ] No quote is taken out of context (the surrounding 2 turns support the interpretation)
- [ ] Quotes are the speaker's own words, not the interviewer's

## Insights & implications
- [ ] Each theme is supported by at least 2 independent signals (quote or observation)
- [ ] Implications are scoped — no over-generalizing from one participant
- [ ] Any insight that touches an open question in decisions.md is cross-referenced
- [ ] "Raw signals" section captures anything notable not yet classifiable

## Quality bar
- [ ] A reader who hasn't listened to the interview would get the same mental model
- [ ] No jargon the participant didn't use themselves
- [ ] Synthesis matches voice.md tone for internal research docs
